About Us

Established in 2010, Mars Logistics Limited has experienced steady growth in the transport, logistics, and clearing industry owing to our investment in state-of-the-art operational infrastructure and strategy.

Mars Logistics was founded by Mr. Bhavesh H. Gohil, a reputable logistics management professional with hands-on experience at all levels of operations within the logistics business.

Today, Mars Logistics serves key clients in both the private and public sectors. With a motivated and qualified staff, a modern fleet of vehicles equipped with on-board tracking systems, and an advanced communication system, we ensure prompt tracking and reporting.

Our current fleet includes 70 skeleton trucks with plans to increase to 100 by the end of 2024. Our dedicated team of 120 staff is also expected to double within the same period.

Our Commitment

We are committed to our customers, our staff, and the quality of our services.

Our Mission

To offer customer-oriented, quality, and timely transport and logistics services.

Directors’ Profile

Mr. Bhavesh H. Gohil – Chief Executive Officer

Mr. Gohil has over 25 years of experience in logistics and supply chain management. He has worked for reputable firms such as Mombasa Salt Works Ltd (Dawda Group) and Maersk Logistics Ltd — a Danish multinational clearing and forwarding company. His areas of expertise include marketing and sales management, procurement, and fleet management. As CEO, he oversees the overall operations of the company.

Mrs. Dipti Gohil – Finance & Administration Director

Mrs. Gohil has over 13 years of experience in the banking sector and possesses extensive expertise in finance and business administration. She currently serves as the Finance and Administration Director of Mars Logistics Limited.
